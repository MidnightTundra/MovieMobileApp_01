How To

Create an account to begin accessing movies and their details. The app will be able to log movie watch time tied to your account on a per movie basis.

The user can detail their account page with personal information as well as log out of their movie tracker account.

Finally, users will be able to search for movies and tally how many minutes they have watched of a chosen film. Information regarding the movie will populate the Records Form page for displaying movie details and adding watch time to your account.

A Guest account is made on launch for the teacher or anyone testing. Failure to login will automatically log in the guest account.

To log in as a guest. Enter these credentials at log in:

Username: Guest

Password: 2345
